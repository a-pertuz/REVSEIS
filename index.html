Welcome to REV-SEIS

REV-SEIS helps geologists and geophysicists breathe new life into old seismic data originally recorded on paper.

With SEGYRecover you can digitize seismic sections into SEG-Y format, VelRecover allows you to interpolate sparse velocity picks, and InSeis helps enhance vintage seismic data through Seismic Unix processes, making it all accessible for modern interpretation.
